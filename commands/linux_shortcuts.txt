SHORTCUTS

TERMINATOR

	1 Split terminator window
		- Vertical: CTRL + SHIFT + O
		- Horizontal: CTRL + SHIFT + E
		- Close split: CTRL + SHIFT + W

	2 Active split: ALT + UP/DOWN/SIDE

    3 Re-size window
        - CRTL + Shift + SIDE
